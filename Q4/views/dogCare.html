Health Care

  • Get your dog vaccinated. Recommended vaccines include:
    • canine adenovirus-2 (hepatitis)
    • canine distemper virus
    • canine parainfluenza virus
  • Register your dog with a local Veterinarian
  • Maintain annual wellness visits with your vet
  • Remain within a healthy weight limit
  • Brush your dogs teeth, maintaining good oral health is important

Dietary Information

  • discuss with your vet to help you choose the best diet for your pup
  • Stear clear of overly processed foods
  • Make sure your feeding your pup the correct amount for his size and breed

House Prep

  • Choose easy to clean areas for your dogs new bed, and water and food dishes
  • Hide electrical cords and wires
  • Clear your house of any toxic products and keep dangerous items out of reach
  • Purchase good quality food
  • Purchase a collar, id tags and a leash
  • Additionally, you'll want to get treats and toys for your new house mate
